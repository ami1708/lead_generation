ReviveIQ — Product Flow
April 2026
"An engine that tracks real-time data about company metrics — funding, growth signals, job postings, and more — then generates a score and notifies you when it's the ideal time to reach out."

What it is

ReviveIQ takes your pile of dead/cold leads, scores each one using Claude AI and live web data, and gives your calling team a ranked list — hottest leads at the top — so they stop calling blind and start calling smart.

The problem

Every sales team has a graveyard of leads who said "follow up in 3 months" and were forgotten. Right now, the process is:

  1. Every 6 months, dump all cold leads on the calling team with zero context
  2. Team calls in random order — a ₹50K deal treated the same as a ₹50L one
  3. Most leads are never contacted at all
  4. Hot leads sit at the bottom of the list, untouched

No prioritisation. No intelligence. Revenue just leaks.

How ReviveIQ works

01 Connect your CRM

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, Teamgate. Dead leads pull in automatically. CSV upload also available.

02 AI searches the web per lead
  • Did the company raise funding recently?
  • Are they hiring for roles that need your product?
  • Are they actively researching similar tools?
  • Are they growing or expanding?
03 Every lead gets a score (1–10)
  • How long ago they went cold
  • Why they went cold — budget constraint vs. rejected
  • Whether a budget was mentioned
  • How engaged they were before going cold
  • Web signals above add up to +3 bonus points
"Went cold 8 months ago due to budget timing. Company just raised Series A. High revival likelihood."
04 Ranked list for the calling team
8–10HOT — call this week
5–7WARM — call this month
1–4COLD — low priority
05 Stays current automatically

Every week: fresh web search per lead, entire list re-scored. If a lead's score jumps 3+ points, manager gets an immediate email alert.

What the manager sees

  • Dashboard with ranked lead table — Hot / Warm / Cold counts at the top
  • Click any lead: score breakdown, web signals found, reason in plain English
  • Weekly email digest: top 10 hot leads every Monday
  • Instant alert when a lead's score spikes
  • Export to CSV for the calling team

What this is NOT (yet)

  • Not sending WhatsApp messages or emails to leads — Phase 2
  • Not generating call scripts or proposals — Phase 2
  • Not live call transcription or conversation intelligence — future

Phase 1 is purely: score the leads, surface the best ones, keep it current.

Tech — one line

CRM pulls leads → Tavily searches web per lead → Claude AI scores using a rubric → dashboard shows ranked results → Resend emails weekly digest

Go-to-market

Pilot on our own sales team first. Recover 10–15 dead leads into paying customers. Use that as the case study. Then sell to other sales teams with proof: "We recovered ₹X from leads we'd already written off."
